How to make a Sweet Lasagna

Description

Making a lasagna doesn't have to be a scary adventure


Follow these easy steps you'll have an amazing meal that will leave you wanting seconds


Ingredients:

  • Bechamel sauce-250 grams of butter
  • Bechamel sauce-3dl milk
  • Bechamel sauce-5 grams salt
  • Bechamel sauce-50grams flour
  • 1 packet of noodles
  • 750grams ground beef
  • 500grams crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 250 grams parmesan cheese
  • 250 grams shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 15 grams basil
  • 15 grams cummin
  • 15 grams chili flakes
  • 15 grams oregano
  • 1 9x13 deep pan

Steps: preheat oven to 185 degrees Celsius

  1. Bechamel sauce: melt the butter into a sauce pot, add milk once it is melted.
  2. Bechamel sauce: bring milk to a boil, add salt and whisk in flour.
  3. Bechamel sauce: lower temp down to simmer, whisk constantly until smooth and creamy.
  4. Bechamel sauce: once the sauce is thick and creamy, remove fom heat and set aside
  5. Meat sauce: fry ground beef in large pot add in crushed garlic cloves and spices
  6. Meat sauce: Once the meat is browned add the crushed tomatoes, reduce heat and simmer
  7. layer the first row of lasagna plates on the bottom of the 9x13 pan
  8. layer the meat, shredded cheese and Bechamel sauce, covered by lasagna plates until the pan is topped off
  9. Top off the pan with parmesan cheese
  10. Cook in oven for 35min
  11. Remove, let settle while making a side salad and enjoy

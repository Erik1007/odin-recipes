How to make mouth watering burgers

Description

Burgers are the meal that can be plain and still be good, but they can also be memorable and awesome!


With a few simple additions, you can Wow your friends with amazing burgers!

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of Swedish 'Max Frisco' burger buns
  • 750 grams of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion (red) finely chopped
  • 5 dashes Cholula Sweet Habanera sauce
  • 1 small yellow onion ring sliced
  • ground salt and pepper
  • 15 grams chili flakes
  • 15 grams cummin spice
  • 15 grams paprika spice
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • Condiments: Ketchup, mustard
  • fry up 250grams of bacon *optional
  • sliced tomatoes, avocados, pickles *optional

Steps: Fire up the grill or warm up the frying pan

  1. In a large mixing bowl, thoroughly combine the ground beef, spices, egg, chopped red onion, salt and pepper, and cholula sauce
  2. Gather palm sized balls of the meat mixture that gets made into flattened patties, set aside
  3. Place the burger patties on teh heat source of your choice.
  4. While the burgers are cooking, slice the onions and get your condiments prepped
  5. Cook to your preference and enjoy

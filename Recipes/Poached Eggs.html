How to Make Perfect Poached Eggs

Description

They look harder than they are!


Give it a go and make the Perfectpoached eggs

Ingredients

  • 1 large pot of simmering water
  • 10ml of vinegar
  • 6 eggs
  • Toast or hard bread
  • Avocado
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Slotted spoon

Steps: Time to take the plunge

  1. Crack an egg into a separate bowl
  2. Put the vinegar into the simmering water
  3. Place slotted spoon just in the water and slide egg onto it
  4. Dip the spoon under the water with a swirl
  5. Allow the egg to stay in boiling water (the longer the more solid the yolk)
  6. Spoon off any excess egg white floating on the surface
  7. I do 1 egg at a time, but feel free to be adventurous
  8. Repeat as many times as you have eggs
  9. Prep the bread/Toast with your choice (butter, avocado, salt and pepper)
  10. Remove the egg and slide it onto the bread and enjoy
